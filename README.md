Mine dewatering is essential to maintain safe and efficient operations in open-pit and underground mining. ## ‚úÖ The Solution: Solar-Hybrid Dewatering System
We propose a **hybrid solar PV + battery + diesel backup system** delivered under an **OPEX (PPA/lease) model**. This ensures 24/7 reliable pumping while minimizing fuel consumption and emissions.

### Key Components
- **High-Efficiency PV Array**: Ground-mounted solar modules sized to meet daily pumping needs.
- **Electric Submersible Pumps with VFDs**: Enable soft start, sleep mode, and water level-based control.
- **MPPT Inverter/Controllers**: Optimize solar power utilization at all times.
- **Battery Energy Storage (BESS)**: Provides power during night/cloudy periods, reducing diesel dependency.
- **Diesel/Hybrid Backup**: Runs only when solar + battery cannot meet demand.
- **Smart Monitoring & Control (PLC/SCADA)**: Enables remote telemetry, predictive maintenance, and automated operation.
