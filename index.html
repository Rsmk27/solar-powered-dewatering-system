The High Cost of Conventional Dewatering

Traditional mine dewatering is a critical operational bottleneck, plagued by high costs, environmental damage, and unreliability, especially in remote locations where the grid is unstable or nonexistent.

High & Volatile OPEX

Extreme dependency on diesel fuel leads to unpredictable expenses, further inflated by the complex logistics of transporting fuel to remote mining sites.

Environmental Impact

Diesel pumps are major sources of CO₂, NOx, and particulate emissions, creating a large carbon footprint that conflicts with modern ESG goals and sustainability targets.

Poor Reliability

Frequent breakdowns, power interruptions, and constant maintenance of diesel engines create a high risk of pit flooding and costly, unplanned production downtime.

A Paradigm Shift: Dewatering-as-a-Service

We don't sell pumps; we deliver guaranteed uptime and water removal through a revolutionary OPEX-based service model. This shifts the financial and operational burden from you to us.

Zero Upfront Capital (CapEx)

Vendor-Managed O&M

Our team handles all operations, maintenance, and repairs. This reduces your in-house maintenance burden and ensures the system runs at peak efficiency, backed by our expertise.

Performance-Based SLAs

Our success is tied to yours. We operate under strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs) that guarantee uptime and pumped volume, aligning our incentives directly with your operational needs.

Intelligent Hybrid Technology

Our mobile units are built on a robust hybrid architecture, combining the best of solar, battery storage, and smart controls to ensure uninterrupted, 24/7 dewatering.

Core System Architecture

  • High-Efficiency PV Array: Sized to meet your daily energy demand, serving as the primary power source.
  • Energy Storage (BESS): A Battery Energy Storage System stores excess solar power for use at night or on cloudy days, minimizing backup generator usage.
  • Smart Pump Control: Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs) optimize pump speed to match water inflow, saving energy and reducing wear.
  • Automated Backup: A small diesel generator provides ultimate reliability, engaging automatically only when solar and battery reserves are depleted.
  • Remote SCADA Monitoring: We monitor the entire system 24/7 via the cloud for real-time telemetry, alarms, and predictive maintenance.

Our Smart Control Prototype

Our innovation is proven. We developed a fully functional prototype demonstrating our core smart-control logic.

  • Automated Sun Tracking: Using Light-Dependent Resistors (LDRs) and a servo motor, our system automatically tilts the solar panel to follow the sun, maximizing energy capture by up to 25%.
  • Sensor-Driven Pumping: An integrated soil moisture sensor (analogous to a water level sensor in a mine) triggers the pump automatically, ensuring it only runs when needed.

Risk & Mitigation Strategy

We've engineered our solution and service model to proactively address the inherent challenges of remote mining operations.

Intermittent Solar Power

Mitigation: Our hybrid design with oversized PV arrays, battery storage, and automated diesel backup ensures continuous power, supplemented by solar forecasting to optimize energy use.

Mechanical Pump Failure

Mitigation: Predictive maintenance using vibration and temperature sensors, combined with a vendor-managed inventory of on-site spares, minimizes downtime.

Telemetry Loss in Remote Sites

Mitigation: We use redundant telecom paths (4G/Satellite) and local data logging with a store-and-forward mechanism to ensure no data is ever lost.

Site Security & Asset Theft

Mitigation: All assets are protected by robust fencing, 24/7 CCTV monitoring, GPS asset tagging, and are fully covered by our comprehensive insurance policy.
