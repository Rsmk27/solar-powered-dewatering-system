The Challenge

The High Cost of
Conventional Dewatering

Traditional mine dewatering is a operational bottleneck, plagued by high costs, environmental damage, and unreliability.

High & Volatile OPEX

Extreme dependency on diesel fuel leads to unpredictable expenses, inflated by complex logistics of transporting fuel to remote sites.

Environmental Impact

Diesel pumps are major sources of CO₂, NOx, and particulate emissions, creating a large carbon footprint that conflicts with ESG goals.

Poor Reliability

Frequent breakdowns and constant maintenance of diesel engines create high risks of pit flooding and costly downtime.

Technology

Intelligent Hybrid Architecture

Smart & Sustainable Core

Our mobile units combine solar power, battery storage, and smart controls for 24/7 dewatering.

  • High-Efficiency PV Array
    Sized to meet daily energy demand as primary source.
  • Energy Storage (BESS)
    Stores excess solar power for night/cloudy operation.
  • Smart Pump Control (VFD)
    Optimizes pump speed to match water inflow.
  • Remote SCADA Monitoring
    24/7 real-time telemetry and predictive maintenance.

Proven Prototype Innovation

We developed a fully functional prototype demonstrating our core logic.

  • Automated Sun Tracking
    Tilts panels to follow the sun, increasing energy capture by up to 25%.
  • Sensor-Driven Pumping
    Integrated soil moisture sensors trigger pumps automatically only when needed.
